Python has over 100 special [[Method]]s that let developers customize the behavior of their [[Object]]s. These [[Method]]'s names are surrounded by double underscores (e.g., `__init__`, `__new__`, `__str__`, `__eq__`, etc.). Noticeable ones:
* [[Initializer]]
* `__str__` and `__repr__`: used to provide human-readable output from values
* [[Comparison method]]s
* [[Arithmetic method]]s

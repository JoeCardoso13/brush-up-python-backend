Can be also referred to as an [[Identifier]].

They are simply **identifiers** of data. They're dynamically [[Type]]d, i.e. there's nothing intrinsically in a variable that tells you from which [[Type]] its value belong. Furthermore, there's no syntactic difference between [[Variable initialization]] and [[Variable reassignment]]:
```python
foo = 'Hello, world.'         # Assignment or initialization
foo = "That's all, folks!"    # Reassignment
```

There's a shorthand notation for the [[Statement]] where a variable is utilized and undergoing [[Variable reassignment]] at the same time: [[Augmented assignment]].

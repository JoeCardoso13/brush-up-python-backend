Is anything that gets evaluated and returns a value. Examples:
- [[Literal]]s: `5`, `'Karl'`, `3.141592`, `True`, `None`
- [[Variable]] references: `foo` or `name` when these variables have been previously defined.
- [[Arithmetic operation]]: `x + y` or `a * b - 5`.
- [[Comparison]] operations: `'x' == 'x'` or `'x' < 'y'`.
- [[String]] operations: `'x' + 'y'` or `'x' * 32`.
- [[Function call]]s: `print('Hello')` or `len('Python')`.
- Any valid combination of the above that evaluates to a single [[Object]].

Expressions can be part of [[Statement]]s, but **not** vice-versa.
